1. About this software

Radar Vital is an academic research system developed by Lemuel Blaya, Angelo Diaz, and Blessie Mugat, students of the BS Electronics Engineering program of the University of Mindanao, Davao City, Philippines (the "Researchers"). It monitors heart rate, respiratory rate, and presence using a Seeed MR60BHA2 60 GHz mmWave radar and presents them in a dashboard for research and training purposes.

2. Not a medical device

Radar Vital is a thesis research instrument. It is not a medical device, has not been evaluated by the Philippine FDA or any regulatory body, and must not be used for diagnosis, treatment, or any clinical decision-making.

Readings are experimental and may be inaccurate. Always rely on clinically validated equipment and qualified professionals for health decisions.
